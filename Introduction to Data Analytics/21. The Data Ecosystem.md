## The Data Ecosystem
A data analyst ecosystem includes the infrastructure, software, tools, frameworks, and processes used to gather, clean, analyze, mine, and visualize data.

### Types of Data
Based on how well-defined the structure of the data is, data is broadly categorized into three entities:
- **Structured Data:** Data that follows a rigid format and can be organized neatly into rows and columns is structured data. This is the data that you see typically in databases and spreadsheets, for example:
  - Dates
  - Phone numbers
  - ZIP codes
  - Customer names

- **Semi-Structured Data:** Semi-structured data is a mix of data that has consistent characteristics and data that doesn’t conform to a rigid structure, it is partially organized and partially free form. For example: emails. An email has a mix of structured data, such as the name of the sender and recipient, but also has the contents of the email, which is unstructured data.
